Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about EduHeTech

Earning Points

How do I earn points on EduHeTech?

You can earn points through multiple activities:

  • Complete courses: 2000 points each
  • Watch ads: 50-200 points per ad
  • Read blog articles: 100 points each
  • Refer friends: 1000 points per referral
  • Spin daily wheel: 50-500 points
  • Daily login bonus: 100 points

Is there a daily limit on how many points I can earn?

Yes, there's a daily limit of 10,000 points from regular activities. However, referral bonuses do NOT count toward this limit, so you can earn unlimited points through referrals!

How often can I spin the daily wheel?

You can spin the wheel once every 24 hours. The timer resets at midnight local time.

Do I need to watch the entire ad to get points?

Yes, you must watch the entire ad duration to receive points. Closing the ad early or navigating away will result in no points being awarded.

Withdrawals & Payments

What is the minimum withdrawal amount?

The minimum withdrawal amount is 500,000 points, which equals $5. This ensures processing fees don't significantly impact your earnings.

How long do withdrawals take to process?

Withdrawals typically process within 24-48 hours. During high volume periods, it may take up to 72 hours. You'll receive a notification once processed.

What payment methods do you support?

We currently support:

  • Bank Transfer
  • PayPal
  • Mobile Money (various providers)
  • Gift Cards (selected retailers)

Why was my withdrawal request rejected?

Common reasons for rejection include:

  • Insufficient points balance
  • Incorrect payment details
  • Suspicious activity detected
  • Violation of terms of service

Contact support if you believe your rejection was in error.

Account & Technical Issues

I forgot my password. How can I reset it?

Click on "Forgot Password" on the login page. Enter your email address, and we'll send you a password reset link. Check your spam folder if you don't see the email.

Can I use multiple devices with one account?

Yes, you can access your account from multiple devices. However, you cannot be logged in on multiple devices simultaneously for security reasons.

Why are my points not updating after completing a course?

Points may take a few minutes to update. If points haven't appeared after 15 minutes:

  • Refresh the page
  • Check your internet connection
  • Ensure you completed all course requirements
  • Contact support with course details

How do I delete my account?

To delete your account, please contact our support team. Note that account deletion is permanent and will result in loss of all points and earnings.

General Questions

Is EduHeTech completely free to use?

Yes! EduHeTech is completely free to use. We offer a premium option that removes daily limits and provides additional features, but the basic platform remains free.

Can I use EduHeTech outside of Nigeria?

Yes, EduHeTech is available worldwide. However, payment methods and currency conversion rates may vary by country.

How do I contact customer support?

You can contact our support team through:

  • Email: support@eduhetech.com
  • In-app chat support
  • Social media channels
  • Contact form on our website

Are the courses on EduHeTech certified?

Many of our courses are from certified platforms and provide completion certificates. However, the certification depends on the specific course provider. Check individual course details for certification information.
