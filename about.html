Our Story

From a simple idea to a platform transforming thousands of lives

The Beginning

EduHeTech was founded in 2023 by a team of educators, technologists, and entrepreneurs who recognized a fundamental problem: millions of people wanted to learn new skills but couldn't afford the time or money investment.

We asked ourselves: "What if learning could pay for itself? What if every minute spent studying could also generate income?" This simple question led to the creation of EduHeTech - a platform where education meets earning.
